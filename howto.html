How to Order

Follow these simple steps to bring your 3D creations to life with us.

1

Submit Your Design

Send us your 3D model file (e.g., .STL, .OBJ) and your printing requirements via WhatsApp or Email. Don't have a design? We can help with that too!

WhatsApp Us Email Us
2

Review Your Quote

We will analyze your design and send you a detailed quote, including the estimated cost and completion time, typically within a few business hours or in the same day.

Alternatively, you can use our Instant Quotation Estimator to get a quick estimate.

Instant Quotation
3

Confirm & Pay

Once you approve the quote, you can confirm your order by making the payment. We'll get the printers running as soon as it's confirmed.

4

Printing & Delivery

Your design will be printed with precision and care. We'll notify you once it's ready for pickup or and the further details for delivery.

Good to Know

A few important things to note before you order.

File Formats

We primarily accept .STL, .OBJ, and .3MF files. If you have a different format, please contact us, and we'll see what we can do.

Materials & Colors

We offer a wide range of materials and colors to suit your project needs. Head over to our Filaments page to explore the options.

Turnaround Time

Standard orders are typically completed within 1-5 business days. This can vary depending on the complexity, size, and our current order queue. We will provide a more precise estimate in your quote.

Customization & Advanced Settings

Learn about our printing standards and how to customize your order.

Tailor Your Prints

We use optimized default settings for a balance of speed and quality. However, you can request specific adjustments like layer height, infill density, and patterns. For detailed information on these options, please review our technical guide or let us know your requirements when ordering.
